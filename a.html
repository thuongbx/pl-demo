General Waste FAQs

Shouldn't I be recycling instead?

We work to encourage businesses to recycle as much as possible - that's why we offer over 20 options of recycling services.

However, sometimes recycling cannot replace general waste disposal. For this our commercial waste collection service is for anything leftover that can't be recycled.

What happens to business waste after you've collected it?

Following the general waste collection service, the general waste is sorted to remove any possible recyclables.

After sorting, your business waste is transported to an Energy from Waste facility, where it's safely incinerated.

The process generates electricity and heat that power homes in the UK and by-products residues are used in the construction industry.

Green, safe and reliable - everything you need to responsibly manage commercial waste from your business.

Do you provide general waste bins?

When you use First Mile's waste management services, you can choose to be provided with a supply of waste sacks or a large general waste bin. For more details, see our commercial bins page.

How soon can I set up a waste collection for waste disposal?

You can set up a general waste collection as soon as you need. Use the options on the right to choose your services, or click here to book a one-off waste collection, rubbish removal or waste disposal.
